Mastering Compost for Richer Soil

Composting is a simple, natural way to recycle organic waste and create a nutrient-rich supplement for your garden soil.

  • Balance Greens & Browns: Mix nitrogen-rich 'greens' with carbon-rich 'browns'.
  • Keep it Moist: Your compost pile should be as damp as a wrung-out sponge.
  • Aerate Regularly: Turn the pile with a pitchfork every week or two to provide oxygen.
